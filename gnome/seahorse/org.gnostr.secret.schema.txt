Schema: org.gnostr.Key
Attributes:
- type=nostr-key
- npub=<npub1...>
- uid=<friendly identity label>
- curve=secp256k1
- origin=software|hardware
- hardware_slot=<token-specific locator, optional>

Label format shown in Seahorse:
  "Nostr key: [display_name or uid] (npub1...)"

Secrets:
- Software keys: store the private key material (hex or nsec) as the item secret
- Hardware keys: do not store private material; store a small reference and set origin=hardware
